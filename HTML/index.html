THE FEW. THE PROUD.

Join Sam's Corps, the official SNHU student army hub. We are more than just a group; we are a powerful community where students unite to support each other and grow. Our hub provides the resources and camaraderie you need to be successful, creating a strong army of future leaders.

On campus, Sam's Corps is your central command for a wide range of activities designed to sharpen your skills and build connections. We host weekly collaborative study sessions in the library, lead tech workshops to master new skills, and organize exciting hackathons to tackle real-world problems. Beyond academics, we strengthen our bonds through on-campus community service projects and social events, ensuring you have the support and friendship needed to thrive at SNHU.

OUR MISSION

Sam's Corps serves as a formidable force-in-readiness, meticulously prepared to respond to academic and community challenges across campus and beyond. Our members are trained to fight and win in a variety of situations, from collaborative projects and problem-solving initiatives to leading their peers. Just as an expeditionary force is ready to deploy and sustain itself anywhere in the world, Sam's Corps is built on a foundation of versatility and adaptability. We are a self-sufficient group that can be relied upon to deliver results, ensuring our community remains strong and our members are prepared for any challenge that comes their way.
